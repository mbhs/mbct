Who We Are

Mission

Our mission as the Montgomery Blair Computer Team is to spread ideas from higher education computer science, especially those of algorithmic problem solving, to fellow students in an accessible format. We explore complex algorithms and data structures not only to succeed in rigorous programming competitions but also to discover exciting opportunities and develop the universally applicable mindset of striking a balance between efficiency and effectiveness in everything we do.
